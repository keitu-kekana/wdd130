Purpose

When was the last time you had a good adreneline rush? You probably don't remember. With us you get a splashing good time with your close friends on a good old-fashioned adventure. Join us and be one with nature and sneak in some exercise while looking fear in the face!

Our services are perfect for groups of people; ages 12-50; looking for some bonding time and good memories. Our website is easily accesible on all devices to find our prices, location and operating hours. We also take online enquiries and quotations with an online bot to assist you.
